## Question

The original research question.

## Answer

Concise direct answer. Should be detailed enough for an agent to gain
actionable knowledge and design technical roadmaps. Include specific
quantitative results, method names, and practical recommendations.

## Supporting Claims

- **[high]** Claim text with specific numbers and method details. (evidence: evidence-id-1)
- **[medium]** Claim text referencing the source. (evidence: evidence-id-2)
- **[low]** Tentative claim with weak support. (evidence: evidence-id-3)

## Inferences

- Practical recommendation or technical roadmap step derived from the evidence.
- Engineering judgment about trade-offs or architecture choices.
- Warning about common pitfalls based on patterns across sources.

## Uncertainty

- Known limitation or caveat that affects the reliability of the answer.
- Area where evidence is weak, conflicting, or absent.

## Missing Evidence

- Specific evidence gap that would strengthen the answer if filled.
- Type of experiment, comparison, or data that is needed but not found.

## Suggested Next Steps

1. Concrete actionable step ordered by priority.
2. Next step building on the first.
3. Advanced or optional step for later.
